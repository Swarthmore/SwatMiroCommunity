Documentation

Admin roles & privileges:

  • Create categories
  • Add videos to categories
  • Create playlists
  • Configure general site settings
  • Moderate videos in moderation queue
  • Add new user &/or admin accounts

Creating Categories:

  1. Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  2. Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  3. Click on ‘Videos’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  4. Click on the ‘Categories’ option at the top of the ‘Videos’ page
  5. Click the ‘Add Category’ button
    6. Input the information for your new category i.e. Category Name, Category Slug, Thumbnail, Description, Parent
  6. Click ‘Add Category’ to save your new Category

Adding Videos to Categories:

  1. Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  2. Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  3. Click on ‘Videos’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  4. Click on the ‘Bulk Edit’ option at the top of the ‘Videos’ page
  5. Locate the video you would like to categorize
  6. Click the ’Edit’ link under the video you want to categorize, and a small window should titled ‘Editing’ should pop up
  7. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and ‘check’ the categories you want the video to be in
  8. Click the ‘Save Changes’ button to save your changes

Creating Playlists:

  1. Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  2. Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  3. Click on ‘Goodies’ at the bottom of the left hand panel on the ‘Admin’ page
  4. Click on the ‘Playlists’ option at the top of the ‘Goodies’ page
  5. Click the ‘Add Playlist’ button and a page titled ‘Add a Playlist’ should pop up
  6. Input the information for your new playlist i.e. Name, Slug, Description
  7. Click the ‘Add Playlist’ button to save your new playlist

Configuring General Site Settings:

  1. Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  2. Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  3. Click on ‘Settings’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  4. Modify the settings to suit your needs i.e. change Site Title, add Site Tagline, Logo Image, Background, Custom CSS, About Us Page, Sidebar Blurb, Footer Blurb.
  5. Configure Additional Site Settings at the bottom of the page if necessary
  6. Click ‘Save Changes’ to save your changes

Moderate Videos In Moderation Queue

  1. Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  2. Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  3. Click on ‘Videos’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  4. Click the the ‘Approve All Videos on this page’ button, ‘Reject All Videos on This Page’ button or ‘Clear Entire Queue’ button to carry out a bulk video moderation
  5. Otherwise, mouse over each video in the queue and select ‘Feature’, ‘Approve’, ‘Reject’ for individual video moderation

Add New User/Admin Accounts:

  1. Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  2. Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  3. Click on ‘Users’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  4. Click the ‘Add User’ button
  5. Input the new user’s information and specify if the account is an admin account or not
  6. Click the ‘Add User’ button to save the new user’s information
