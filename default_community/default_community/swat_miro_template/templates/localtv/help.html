Admin Documentation

Admin roles & privileges:

  • Create categories
  • Add videos to categories
  • Create playlists
  • Configure general site settings
  • Moderate videos in moderation queue
  • Add new user &/or admin accounts

Creating Categories:

  • Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  • Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  • Click on ‘Videos’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  • Click on the ‘Categories’ option at the top of the ‘Videos’ page
  • Click the ‘Add Category’ button
    • Input the information for your new category i.e. Category Name, Category Slug, Thumbnail, Description, Parent
  • Click ‘Add Category’ to save your new Category

Adding Videos to Categories:

  • Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  • Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  • Click on ‘Videos’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  • Click on the ‘Bulk Edit’ option at the top of the ‘Videos’ page
  • Locate the video you would like to categorize
  • Click the ’Edit’ link under the video you want to categorize, and a small window should titled ‘Editing’ should pop up
  • Scroll down to the bottom of the page and ‘check’ the categories you want the video to be in
  • Click the ‘Save Changes’ button to save your changes

Creating Playlists:

  • Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  • Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  • Click on ‘Goodies’ at the bottom of the left hand panel on the ‘Admin’ page
  • Click on the ‘Playlists’ option at the top of the ‘Goodies’ page
  • Click the ‘Add Playlist’ button and a page titled ‘Add a Playlist’ should pop up
  • Input the information for your new playlist i.e. Name, Slug, Description
  • Click the ‘Add Playlist’ button to save your new playlist

Configuring General Site Settings:

  • Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  • Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  • Click on ‘Settings’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  • Modify the settings to suit your needs i.e. change Site Title, add Site Tagline, Logo Image, Background, Custom CSS, About Us Page, Sidebar Blurb, Footer Blurb.
  • Configure Additional Site Settings at the bottom of the page if necessary
  • Click ‘Save Changes’ to save your changes

Moderate Videos In Moderation Queue

  • Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  • Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  • Click on ‘Videos’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  • Click the the ‘Approve All Videos on this page’ button, ‘Reject All Videos on This Page’ button or ‘Clear Entire Queue’ button to carry out a bulk video moderation
  • Otherwise, mouse over each video in the queue and select ‘Feature’, ‘Approve’, ‘Reject’ for individual video moderation

Add New User/Admin Accounts:

  • Click on your username in the top right hand corner of the screen
  • Select ‘Admin’ in the dropdown menu
  • Click on ‘Users’ in the left hand panel of the ‘Admin’ page
  • Click the ‘Add User’ button
  • Input the new user’s information and specify if the account is an admin account or not
  • Click the ‘Add User’ button to save the new user’s information

Swarthmore Miro Community End User Documentation

Purpose The purpose of Swarthmore’s Miro Community is to curate various media for classroom use. Student projects can be showcased and teaching materials made readily available.

End User Privileges

Authenticated, non-admin end users can do the following:

  • View and comment on videos
  • Create and submit videos to private playlists
  • Submit admin-approved videos to the website for display
  • Create a profile and change Email notification settings

Logging in and out

  • Click “Login” in the upper-right corner of the screen
  • Click “Swarthmore Login” and enter your network credentials
  • Notice that the “Login” option is now a drop-down menu
  • This menu contains links to your profile and to logging out

Viewing and Commenting on Videos

  • To view a video, click on its thumbnail
  • To leave a comment on a video, use the Disqus commenting mechanism below the video
  • Requires login credentials

Submitting a Video

  • Requires login credentials
  • Direct Submission
  • In the upper-right corner of the screen, click “Submit”
  • Input the video URL and click “Submit”
  • If an admin approves the video, it will be displayed in the video grid
  • Category Submission: submission link method
  • If an admin provides a submission link, just click the link and submit a video URL as if following the direct submission method
  • Category Submission: category page submission method
  • Click “Browse” in upper-right hand corner of screen.
  • Click either “Categories” or any of the categories listed underneath
  • Click the “Submit to this Category” button and follow the steps for direct submission.

Creating a Playlist

  • Requires login credentials
  • From the main page (the video grid), click “Browse” in the upper right area of the screen.
  • Click “Playlists”
  • Click “Add Playlist,” fill out the fields in the window that appears, and click the newly-formed “Add Playlist” button.
  • Note: Making playlists public requires admin approval
  • Playlists can be modified with the Edit and View options

Adding Videos to Playlists

  • Requires login credentials
  • Locate the video to be added
  • Expand the drop-down menu and locate the video you want to add
  • Click “Add”
  • After a short time, the page will refresh to confirm video submission to the playlist

Creating a Profile

  • Requires login credentials
  • Log in and click the drop-down menu under your username (upper-right corner of screen)
  • Click “Profile”
  • The profile page should be the default display. Fill in the fields and click “Save Changes.”
  • To view your profile, click “View Profile.” Alternatively, click “About” when viewing the main video grid or the video viewing page.

Changing Email Notification Settings

  • Requires login credentials
  • Follow the steps for creating a profile, but click the “Notifications” tab
  • Toggle the checkboxes to change Email notification settings
